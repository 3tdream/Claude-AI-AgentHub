Quick market scan for: $ARGUMENTS

Deliver in 2-3 minutes of reading time:

1. **MARKET OVERVIEW** — size, growth rate, key trends (2-3 sentences)
2. **KEY PLAYERS** (5-7) — name, market share estimate, positioning
3. **EMERGING TRENDS** (3-5) — what's changing, why it matters
4. **OPPORTUNITIES** (2-3) — gaps in the market, underserved segments
5. **RISKS** (2-3) — threats, regulatory, competition

Keep it concise — this is a scan, not a deep dive. Use `/competitor-dive` for deeper analysis.
Cite specific numbers and sources where possible.

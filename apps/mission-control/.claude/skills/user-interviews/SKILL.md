## INTERVIEW STRUCTURE (30 min)

### Warm-up (5 min)
- 2-3 rapport-building questions about their role/context

### Discovery (10 min)
- 5-7 open-ended questions about current workflow and pain points
- "Walk me through how you currently..."
- "What's the most frustrating part of..."

### Concept Validation (10 min)
- 3-5 questions testing specific assumptions
- Show concepts/mockups if available
- "If you could wave a magic wand..."

### Prioritization (5 min)
- Card sort or ranking exercise
- "Which of these matters most to you?"

## RULES
- No leading questions ("Don't you think X is better?")
- No yes/no questions — always open-ended
- Include follow-up probes for each question
- Note what to OBSERVE (body language, hesitation)

BACKGROUND MUSIC SETUP
======================

To add background music to your presentation:

1. Add your audio file to this 'public' folder with the name:
   - background-music.mp3 (recommended)
   OR
   - background-music.ogg (fallback format)

2. The audio will automatically loop and play in the background

3. A mute/unmute button appears in the top-left corner of the presentation

Note: Due to browser autoplay policies, the audio may not play automatically
until the user interacts with the page. The mute button will show as muted
until the audio starts playing.

Recommended audio formats:
- MP3: Best compatibility
- OGG: Alternative format for better compression
